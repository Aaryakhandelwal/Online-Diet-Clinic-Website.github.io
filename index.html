About Prachi Singhal

Dr. Prachi Singhal is an online/offline clinical dietician and nutritionist. She practices offline in Sector 83, Gurugram, and also takes online consultations. She believes in creating healthy habits, not restrictions, for a better lifestyle.

She holds a Master's degree specializing in Food and Nutrition and an MBA in Hospital Management. She established Zeal Wellness & Diet Solutions in 2012, which started in Dubai, UAE, and now continues in Gurgaon.

She has sound knowledge of various therapeutic diets for different disorders, including cardiovascular diets, diabetic and renal diets, and weight loss /gain. With an experience of 11 years, she believes in making your diet a "best friend" in weight loss and letting your bump indicate satisfaction, a perfect feeling of being light yet fulfilling and nourishing.

Zeal Wellness & Diet Solutions, under the guidance of Prachi Singhal, aims for lifestyle modification and motivate people for more vital physical and mental health. You can view our testimonial, weight loss success stories, and experience of our clients who have successfully reduced weight through their healthy weight loss diet charts.
